# Welcome to Our Blog

We're excited to launch our new website! This blog will be the home for our latest updates, tutorials, and announcements.

## What to Expect

- **Tutorials** — Step-by-step guides on topics we care about
- **Updates** — News about our projects and milestones
- **Insights** — Thoughts on technology, design, and building for the web

## Built with Modern Tools

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build), styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), and content is managed through [Decap CMS](https://decapcms.org) — making it fast, beautiful, and easy to update.

Stay tuned for more posts!
